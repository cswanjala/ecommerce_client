Brewing the Perfect Herbal Coffee

  1. Add one tablespoon of your chosen blend to a coffee maker, French press, or pour-over setup.
  2. Pour hot water over the blend and steep for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Strain, pour, and enjoy! Customize with milk, sweeteners, or spices for an extra touch.
