About PlayChessUp

At PlayChessUp, we combine the ancient game of chess with cutting-edge AI technology to offer a revolutionary chess experience that helps players learn, play, and improve like never before.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To make chess more accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from beginners to experts, using the power of AI. We believe that chess is not just a game, but a powerful tool for learning and personal growth.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We embrace the latest in AI and technology to constantly evolve and improve the chess experience.

Accessibility

We believe chess should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or skill level.

Education

Chess is a powerful educational tool, and we strive to make learning fun and impactful for all players.
